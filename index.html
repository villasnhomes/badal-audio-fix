📊 Multimeter Display Symbols Explained

⎓
DC (Direct Current)
Solid line with dashes below. Used for battery, power supply voltages (12V, 5V rails)
~
AC (Alternating Current)
Wavy line. Used for mains power testing and audio output signals
Ω
RESISTANCE (Ohms)
Used to measure resistors, speaker coils, check for shorts
🔔
CONTINUITY BEEP
Beeps when circuit is complete. Use for tracing PCB tracks and checking fuses
hFE
TRANSISTOR hFE (Gain)
Measures transistor gain. Insert transistor in hFE socket (B, C, E pins). Typical: 100–400
⊣⊢
DIODE TEST
Forward voltage of diode. Silicon: 0.5–0.7V. Use to test diodes and transistor junctions
F / µF
CAPACITANCE
Measures capacitor value. Compare with marked value ±20%. If off, capacitor is bad

🔌 Probe Placement Rules

⬛ BLACK probe → Always to GROUND (chassis, negative terminal, GND point on PCB)
🟥 RED probe → To the point you are measuring (emitter, collector, test point)
⚠️ NEVER reverse probes when measuring DC voltage. Negative reading means reversed polarity!
